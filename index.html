Who are we?

We are a group at the University of Ottawa which is dedicated to extracurricular programming and system administration projects. You can find us on Discord, Instagram, and Github.

  • Discord
  • Instagram
  • GitHub

Code && Coffee

Casual meetup for those who program or are learning how. Demo your projects, discuss the latest tech, or just enjoy a cup of coffee while working. Everyone interested in coding is welcome.

Awesome, but when and where?

Code && Coffee events are organized around twice a month on the uOttawa campus. Join our Discord server to stay updated!

Book Club

Casual weekly meetup done on Discord, discussing a book chapter by chapter. Come learn with us as we dive into programming resilient distributed systems and functional programming.

Awesome, but when and where?

We are currently taking suggestions for books to read. Feel free to suggest a book on our Discord server!

CSGames

CS Games 2026 is coming soon! We are looking for competitive uOttawa students who love to program and want to push their skills to new limits. You haven't heard of CS Games? That's ok. One of the best parts about it is that you don't need any previous hackathon or competition experience. Click here if you want to learn about our previous CS Games teams.
